Why Solar System

  • Solar energy is important for many reasons, including a long project life of more than 25 years, providing reliable and consistent energy generation for decades.
  • Reliable Technology: Solar panels are designed to work efficiently under various weather conditions and require minimal maintenance.
  • System Warranty: Most solar systems come with a warranty of 25 years, ensuring performance and long-term reliability.
  • Government Support: Enjoy subsidies, tax benefits, and depreciation advantages offered by the government, reducing the upfront cost of installation.
  • Environmental Benefits: Solar energy supports a green and clean environment by reducing carbon footprint and dependency on fossil fuels.
  • Cost Savings: Residential, commercial, and industrial users can significantly reduce electricity bills, sometimes up to 90%, depending on system size and consumption.
  • Energy Independence: Solar systems reduce dependence on the grid and provide backup during power outages.
  • Scalable & Flexible: Solar systems can be installed on rooftops, open lands, or industrial spaces, and can be expanded as energy needs grow.

On-grid Systems / Grid Tie Systems & Net Metering

Most widely used systems installed across India. These systems are installed in societies, homes, offices, factories, schools, hospitals, hotels, banks and many more.

Net Metering: Any excess energy exported to the grid can be adjusted in the bill. This ensures maximum savings and efficiency.
