Scheme Highlights

  • Subsidy of ₹30,000 for 1kW, ₹60,000 for 2kW and ₹78,000 for 3kW.
  • Applicable for residential households with valid electricity connections.
  • Simple online application via the National Portal.
  • Transparent vendor ratings and DISCOM integration.
  • Quick installation and monitoring through certified vendors.
  • Long-term maintenance and warranty support.
